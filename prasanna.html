Devolopment of an app for Food waste reduction manegement

An app based on waste food reduction manegement.

Let us discuss the features of the app for reduction of food wastage.

OBJECTIVE

The main objective of this app is to reduce waste manegement.Now a days wasting food has become the casual thing in day to day life.This app helps the user in reduction of food wastage.

FEATURES OF APP

Food waste in functions and parties

Most of the quantity food is wased in daily basis.
To overcome this problem our app is designed on the basis of location.
This app shows the location of nearest orphanages and old age homes,so that the left over food can be distributed to them.

Calculating the quantity of food required

As prevention is better than cure.
This app calculates the quantity of food required for cooking as per the customer required.This also consider the number of items and no of consumers of food

Complete decomposition of waste

Complete Utilisation of food is not possible.However 10%-20% of food like peels of vegetable will get wasted.This app is designed to over this problem in two ways
1.Feeding animals(App provides the assess to the location of nearest cattle farm)
2.Decomposition(Provides the ways of decompositing of waste materials and gives the proper knowledge)

Cyber security thrift

There is no security thrift due to this app and designed in user friendly manner.
